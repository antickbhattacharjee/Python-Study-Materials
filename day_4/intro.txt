🔎 What are Conditional Statements?
Conditional statements allow programs to make decisions and execute different code 
depending on the conditions.

⚙️ Logical Operators in Conditions
and – True if both conditions are true
or – True if at least one is true
not – Inverts the condition

⚠️ Things to remember
Don’t forget the colon (:) at the end of if, elif, else
Indentation is mandatory in Python (usually 4 spaces)
