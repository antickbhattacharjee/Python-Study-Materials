🔧 Types of Operators in Python
Type	                                    Operators	                                        Example
Arithmetic	                            +, -, *, /, //, %, **	                                 a + b
Relational	                             ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=	                                 x > y
Logical	                                   and, or, not	                                x > 10 and x < 20
Assignment	                          =, +=, -=, *=, /=, //=, **=	                             x += 5
Bitwise (Intro)	                          &, `, ^, <<, >>                               5 & 3, 5 ^ 3, 5 >> 1
Membership	                                in, not in	                                     'a' in 'apple'
Identity	                                is, is not	                                         x is y
